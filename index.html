CCT Blog: 4 on-campus beverages & food items you should be enjoying

Shivam

Last Updated: August 23rd, 2024

1. Italian soda at Bytes

New Black & CCET, 2nd Year

When’s the last time you’ve had an Italian soda? It had been a while for me, but I wanted to join my friend for a non-caffeinated beverage at Bytes...

Recommendation: Raspberry Italian soda

2. Bubble tea at Pop Tea Bar

New Black & CCET, 2nd Year

Bubble tea is widely consumed across the world...

Recommendation: Classic Milk Tea with tapioca

3. Latte at CoHo

New Black & CCET, 1st Year

I can’t tell you whether CoHo has a particularly amazing Vanilla Bean Latte, only because CoHo is the only place I’ve ever ordered...

Recommendation: Vanilla Bean Latte

4. Agua Fresca at Nexus Cafe

New Black & CCET, 1st Year

Nexus Cafe has a lot of tasty things packed into its California space, including Agua Fresca!...

Recommendation: Agua Fresca

1. Noodles at Asian Delight

New Black & CCET, 2nd Year

Craving something warm and comforting? The noodles at Asian Delight are a must-try. Packed with fresh veggies and savory sauce...

Recommendation: Spicy Garlic Noodles

2. Sandwich at The Deli Spot

New Black & CCET, 2nd Year

Sometimes, all you need is a classic sandwich to satisfy your hunger. The Deli Spot offers a range of freshly made sandwiches...

Recommendation: Turkey & Avocado Sandwich

3. Egg Omelet at Morning Brew

New Black & CCET, 1st Year

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, and nothing beats a fluffy egg omelet from Morning Brew...

Recommendation: Cheese & Spinach Omelet
